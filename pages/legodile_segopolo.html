What is Peo ?

Introducing Peo Linux OS: a groundbreaking initiative from Botswana designed to usher the nation into the digital age with a homegrown solution. Crafted with local expertise and innovation, Peo Linux OS not only provides a robust and secure operating system but also champions Botswana’s technological sovereignty. This visionary OS empowers businesses, educators, and citizens alike by integrating regional needs and driving digital transformation. As Botswana forges its path towards a connected future, Peo Linux OS stands as a testament to the nation’s commitment to self-reliance and progress..

Cost Savings: Linux is free and open-source, reducing the cost of software for businesses and educational institutions.
Customization: Linux can be tailored to meet specific needs, allowing for localized and customized solutions.
Security: Linux is known for its strong security features, reducing vulnerability to malware and cyber-attacks.
Community Support: A large and active open-source community provides support, resources, and development opportunities.

Benefits of Linux

Local Development: Encourages local development of software and solutions, boosting the tech industry and job creation in Botswana.
Compatibility: Linux supports a wide range of hardware, making it adaptable to various devices and systems.
Innovation: Open-source nature fosters innovation and experimentation, leading to new and creative solutions.
Data Sovereignty: Keeps data within the country, enhancing control and privacy over national and personal information.
